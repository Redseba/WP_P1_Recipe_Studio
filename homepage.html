About

As college students ourselves, we understand the struggle of not being able to cook complicated meals or always ordering takeout between classes, studying, and surviving on a tight budget. That's why we created this site to share simple, affordable, and delicious recipes that any student can whip up in a dorm room or tiny apartment kitchen.
