Crispy Mozzarella Sticks


Main Ingredients

String cheese

All-purpose flour

Eggs

Italian breadcrumbs

Parmesan

Garlic powder

Oregano

Salt

Pepper

Cooking oil


Marinara Sauce (optional, from scratch)

5 lbs fresh roma or plum tomatoes

4 cloves garlic, peeled and halved

2 tbsp olive oil

1 tbsp balsamic vinegar (optional)

2 tsp dried oregano leaves

2 tsp dried basil leaves

1 tsp fine salt

Freshly ground black pepper


Marinara Instructions (skip if not needed)

Step 1: Preheat oven to 425°F.

Step 2: Cut tomatoes in half lengthwise and place in a large roasting pan.

Step 3: Scatter garlic over tomatoes, then drizzle with olive oil and balsamic vinegar.

Step 4: Sprinkle with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Toss to coat evenly.

Step 5: Roast for 60–70 minutes, stirring halfway through, until slightly charred but not dried out.

Step 6: Let cool, then remove from pan using a slotted spoon if there's excess liquid.

Step 7: Blend until you reach your desired texture — smooth, chunky, or a mix of both.


Instructions

Step 1: Slice the cheese sticks into smaller portions if desired.

Step 2: Set up a dipping station with three containers: flour, beaten eggs, and seasoned breadcrumbs.

Step 3: Roll each cheese stick in flour, dip in egg, then press into breadcrumbs. Repeat for an extra crispy coating.

Step 4: Place coated cheese sticks on a baking sheet and freeze for at least 1 hour.

Step 5: Heat oil to 350°F and fry in small batches for 1–2 minutes until golden brown and crispy.

Step 6: Remove with a spoon and drain on paper towels.

Serve with marinara sauce and enjoy!

Stuffed Garlic Bread Bites


Main Ingredients

1 cup garlic cloves

¼ cup fresh parsley

½ cup olive oil

1½ tsp kosher salt

1 tbsp oil

1 pound ground beef (or your choice of meat)

Seasoning

24 mini pizza dough rounds (2–3 inches each)

1 egg, beaten (for egg wash)


Pizza Dough (optional, from scratch)

4 cups all-purpose flour or bread flour

1½ tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

2¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)

1½ cups warm water (105–115°F)

2 tbsp olive oil


Pizza Dough Instructions (skip if not needed)

Step 1: Combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

Step 2: Whisk together flour and salt in a large bowl.

Step 3: Make a well in the flour, pour in yeast mixture and olive oil, and mix until a shaggy dough forms.

Step 4: Knead on a lightly floured surface for 8–10 minutes until smooth, shiny, and elastic.

Step 5: Place in a lightly oiled bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm spot for 1½ hours until doubled.

Step 6: Deflate the dough and turn onto a floured surface.

Step 7: Cut into 24 equal pieces for mini rounds or 2 pieces for regular pizzas.

Step 8: Roll each piece into a smooth ball, cover loosely, and rest for 15–20 minutes.

Step 9: Roll each ball into a thin round on a floured surface.


Instructions

Step 1: Preheat oven to 400°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

Step 2: Blend all garlic pesto ingredients until completely smooth. Set aside.

Step 3: Heat oil in a large frying pan over high heat. Cook ground meat until browned, breaking up lumps.

Step 4: Stir in about ½ cup garlic pesto until well combined. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 5: Whisk together ¼ cup garlic pesto and the beaten egg in a small bowl. Set aside for topping.

Step 6: Brush a thin layer of garlic pesto over one dough round.

Step 7: Place about 1 tbsp of meat mixture in the center of the dough round.

Step 8: Fold the dough around the filling, stretching gently to enclose it completely.

Step 9: Place seam-side down on the baking sheet. Repeat with remaining dough and filling.

Step 10: Brush the pesto-egg mixture generously over the top of each filled round.

Step 11: Bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown.

Serve warm with marinara sauce and enjoy!

Jalapeno Poppers


Main Ingredients

10 jalapeño peppers

8 oz cream cheese, softened

1 cup shredded cheddar cheese

½ tsp garlic powder

½ tsp onion powder

¼ tsp salt

1 cup all-purpose flour (for coating)

2 large eggs, beaten

2 cups seasoned breadcrumbs

Oil for frying


Instructions

Step 1: Put on gloves and cut jalapeños in half lengthwise. Remove seeds and membranes.

Step 2: In a bowl, mix cream cheese, cheddar, garlic powder, onion powder, and salt until smooth.

Step 3: Fill each jalapeño half with the cheese mixture.

Step 4: Set up a dipping station with three bowls: flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs.

Step 5: Coat each filled pepper in flour, dip in egg, then roll in breadcrumbs. Repeat egg and breadcrumb steps for extra crunch.

Step 6: Place coated poppers on a baking sheet and freeze for at least 1 hour to prevent cheese from leaking during frying.

Step 7: Heat oil in a deep fryer or heavy pot to 350°F.

Step 8: Fry in small batches for 2–3 minutes until golden brown and crispy.

Step 9: Drain on paper towels.

Serve warm and enjoy!
