Golden Chick and Waffles


Fried Chicken

4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs or drumsticks

2 cups buttermilk

2 teaspoons hot sauce (optional)

1½ cups all-purpose flour

½ cup cornstarch

1 teaspoon garlic powder

1 teaspoon smoked paprika

1 teaspoon salt

½ teaspoon black pepper

½ teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)

Vegetable oil (for frying)


Waffles

2 cups all-purpose flour

2 tablespoons sugar

1 tablespoon baking powder

½ teaspoon salt

2 large eggs

1¾ cups milk

½ cup unsalted butter, melted and cooled

1 teaspoon vanilla extract


Instructions

Step 1: Marinate chicken in buttermilk and hot sauce for at least 2 hours or overnight.

Step 2: Take chicken out 20–30 minutes before frying.

Step 3: Mix flour, cornstarch, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, pepper, and cayenne in a bowl.

Step 4: Heat oil in a deep skillet or fryer to 350°F.

Step 5: Coat each piece of chicken in the flour mixture, pressing firmly.

Step 6: Fry in small batches for 12–15 minutes, flipping once, until golden (165°F inside). Drain on a wire rack.

Step 7: Preheat and grease waffle iron.

Step 8: Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl.

Step 9: In another bowl, whisk eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla.

Step 10: Combine wet and dry ingredients, stirring gently until just mixed.

Step 11: Pour batter onto waffle iron and cook 4–5 minutes until golden and crisp.

Step 12: Serve chicken on waffles with butter and maple syrup. Try hot sauce or honey for a twist!

Sunny Avocado Toast


Main Ingredients

1 avocado

100g feta cheese, crumbled (optional)

2 eggs

2 thick slices of brown bread

Sea salt and pepper

Topping (optional): cress

Garnish (optional): chili flakes


Ginger and Lemon Oil

4 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

1 tbsp grated ginger

1 tbsp lemon zest

1 tbsp lemon juice


Bread (optional, from scratch)

2¼ tsp active dry yeast

½ cup warm water (105–115°F)

1 pinch granulated sugar or honey

2 cups warm water

¼ cup granulated sugar or honey

1 tbsp salt

¼ cup vegetable oil

6–7 cups bread flour or all-purpose flour

2 tbsp melted butter (for brushing, optional)


Bread Instructions (skip if not needed)

Step 1: Mix warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.

Step 2: Add remaining water, sugar, salt, oil, and 3 cups of flour. Stir to combine.

Step 3: Gradually add more flour ½ cup at a time until dough pulls from the bowl and feels slightly sticky.

Step 4: Knead 5 minutes with a mixer or 5–8 minutes by hand until smooth and elastic.

Step 5: Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise in a warm spot for 1½ hours until doubled.

Step 6: Punch dough down, divide in half, and shape each into a log.

Step 7: Place in greased loaf pans, cover loosely, and let rise 45 minutes to 1 hour.

Step 8: Preheat oven to 350°F. Bake 30–33 minutes until golden and hollow when tapped.

Step 9: Remove from pans, brush with butter if desired, and cool at least 15 minutes before slicing.


Instructions

Step 1: Combine all ginger and lemon oil ingredients in a small bowl and set aside.

Step 2: Bring a small saucepan of water to boil. Halve the avocados, remove pit and skin, and mash with ¾ of the feta.

Step 3: Once boiling, add eggs and simmer 5 minutes for runny yolk or 8 minutes for set yolk. Toast the bread meanwhile.

Step 4: Remove eggs, cool under the tap, peel, and cut in half.

Step 5: Spread avocado and feta mixture onto warm toast and top with boiled egg halves.

Step 6: Sieve the oil mixture to remove lemon and ginger, then drizzle the infusion over the bread to taste.

Step 7: Top with remaining feta and garnish with chili flakes and cress if desired.

Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve with extra oil on the side and enjoy!

Morning Berry Bowl


Main Ingredients

50ml fruit yogurt (strawberry)

50ml fruit yogurt (blueberry)

50ml vanilla yogurt

10g honey granola

10g oat flakes

10g almonds

10g walnuts

3 strawberries

1 tbsp blueberries

1 tbsp raspberries

Fresh mint (optional)


Granola (optional, from scratch)

4 cups rolled oats

1 cup sliced almonds

1 cup chopped pecans

1 cup raw sunflower seeds

⅓ cup canola oil

½ cup honey

1 tbsp ground cinnamon

1 tsp vanilla extract


Granola Instructions (skip if not needed)

Step 1: Preheat oven to 300°F (150°C).

Step 2: Stir oats, almonds, pecans, and sunflower seeds together in a large bowl.

Step 3: Mix oil, honey, cinnamon, and vanilla together in a smaller bowl.

Step 4: Pour wet ingredients over dry and mix well. Spread onto two ungreased baking sheets.

Step 5: Bake until golden, about 20 minutes, stirring halfway through. Let cool completely before using.


Instructions

Step 1: Pour the yogurts into a bowl one at a time.

Step 2: Alternate the fruit, oats, and seeds on top of the yogurt.

Step 3: Garnish with fresh mint if desired.

Your morning berry bowl is ready — enjoy!
